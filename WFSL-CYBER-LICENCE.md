# WFSL Cyber Licence (Internal / Commercial)

This licence governs WFSL Cyber instruments including NIIM.

## Scope
- Evidence-only telemetry
- Deterministic analysis
- Stability scoring
- Incident reporting

## Explicit Prohibitions
- Packet inspection (DPI)
- Signal interception
- Attribution to individuals
- RF, BLE, MAC fingerprinting
- Behavioural inference

## Convergence Rights
WFSL Cyber may integrate with other WFSL products where:
- No prohibited activity is introduced
- Outputs remain evidence-only
- Governance and baselines are preserved

## Enterprise Use
Permitted under contract.
Audit, exit, and suspension clauses mandatory.

## Legal Boundary
Crossing into interception, attribution, or surveillance
requires a separate licensed entity.
